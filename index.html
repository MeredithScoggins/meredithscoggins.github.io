    Better Together: Coffee & Cardamom Dec. 01

    Above left: Hazelnut-chocolate cardamom buns by Green Kitchen Stories


    As the weather gets cooler I start planning all the ways to stay cozy and warm at home. One of my favorite ways to enjoy cold mornings is slowly lingering over coffee, preferably wrapped in a warm blanket.

    To get us excited about the dark, freezing mornings in Finland, we started dedicating Saturday mornings to making something special for breakfast (usually pancakes) and huddling around our tiny kitchen table with coffee. We spent time talking about the week and planning out our day while the pancakes sizzled on the stove. To switch it up we tried different varieties of pancakes. There were blueberry pancakes, banana bread pancakes, apple pancakes, and coconut pancakes. And then one morning there were cardamom buns.

    Now, I would not suggest these if you are just looking for a quick breakfast treat, but if you have some time to spare these buns will not let you down. If you’ve never tried cardamom, well you need to. So go pour yourself a cup of coffee and make these buns. Then freeze half of them and pop one in the oven when you’re in need of a fika. You can thank me later.

    Spain Road trip | Altea, Mojacar, and Guadix Sep. 03

    Our last week in Spain we rented a car and spent a week driving from Valencia to Seville. Our plan was to not have a plan. Or at least a very loose one. We left on Tuesday and had to get to Seville by the next Tuesday, and that was about it. After being carless for the greater part of two years we were really looking forward to the freedom that a car would allow us. We could go anywhere we wanted, whenever we wanted, all without buying tickets! We didn’t even have to pack our suitcases so meticulously because, hey, just throw it in the trunk! The joy.
