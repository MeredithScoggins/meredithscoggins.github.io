Hi, I’m Meredith and I started this blog with my husband, Scott, when we moved to Finland.  We use it to document and share our experiences - the new, exciting and embarrassing ones.  Since we uprooted our lives, we’ve become serious lovers of travel.  So we like to share some of the moments that we get to spend exploring new places.  I take the photos while Scott is the genius behind the technical side of things - we make a pretty good team.
