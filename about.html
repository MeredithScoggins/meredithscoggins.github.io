Hi, we're Meredith and Scott and we started this blog to share our adventures when we moved to Finland in 2013.  During that two year period we got married and traveled all around Europe, experiencing as many places as possible.

From our travels we have had so many belly-aching laughs, anxious beginnings, tearful nights, cultural mishaps, awe-inspiring moments, communication failures, unforgettable memories, and gosh darn delicious food. But most of all we've met some of the most kind-hearted people and made lasting friendships. All of these experiences have opened our minds and hearts to so many new things that have changed us for the better. Traveling invigorates and challenges us in ways that we could never imagine. To say that we love to travel is an understatement, so we're constantly scheming up ways to visit new places. No time to waste.

We love to meet new people and hear from you, so chat us up through email, instagram or twitter!
